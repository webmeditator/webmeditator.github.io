Samikshya Aryal

Esthetician/Threader

Samikshya(Sam) is a professional threader with a decade of experience in threading. After moving from New York in 2013, Sam has gained vital experience working with the most renowned salons in south bay like Image, Angel and Khoobsurat threading salon. Sam also worked for Burke Williams, an upscale spa in Santana Row. She worked in the management side of the spa and have gained very valuable customer service experience in the beauty industry.

Combining the technical and management experience is how Sam’s Eyebrows came along.
